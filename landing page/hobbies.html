My passion for cooking

Food about my country

Spanish cuisine boasts regional delights like Pulpo a la gallega, a Galician specialty featuring octopus seasoned with olive oil, paprika, and salt. The Tortilla de patatas, a golden omelette of eggs, potatoes, and onions, is a culinary gem. Croquettes, with their creamy filling and crispy exterior, are an irresistible treat across Spain. Originating from Cordoba, Salmorejo is a refreshing cold soup made with tomatoes, bread, olive oil, and garlic. These dishes encapsulate the diverse and charming flavors of Spanish cuisine, captivating taste buds with their authentic and traditional essence.

Favourite type of food

In addition to the food of my country, I like Turkish food very much, it is a very spicy and flavourful type of food and I like the combinations with yoghurt.

My special recipe

My special recipe is pasta pizza, it's like ravioli, but the sauce has fried tomato, melted cheese and sundried tomatoes, and then I finish with a little oregano, giving it the characteristic flavour of its name.
